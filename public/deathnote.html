Death Note

Death Note follows Light Yagami, a high school student who discovers a Death Note, a supernatural notebook allowing him to kill anyone by writing their name. Determined to create a utopia by eliminating criminals, Light faces off against the brilliant detective L in a suspenseful battle of wits. The series explores morality, justice, and the consequences of wielding absolute power in a captivating narrative.
